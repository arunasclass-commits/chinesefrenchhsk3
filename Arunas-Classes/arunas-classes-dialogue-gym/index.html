How each mode helps

🎙️ Shadow — hear the model, record yourself, compare back-to-back. The fastest fix for tones.
🎭 Roleplay — the app plays the other person; you say your line from memory. Speech recognition checks you before you advance.
🎵 Tone Pairs — a 2-minute ear-and-mouth drill on minimal pairs (买/卖) with pitch curves.
🔁 Sentence SRS — spaced repetition on whole sentences you must produce, not just read.
